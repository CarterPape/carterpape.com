## profile

I am a reporter for _American Banker_ living in Sacramento, California. I have previously reported from Utah, Colorado, and North Carolina.

I have seven years of professional experience in the field of journalism, one year of experience in student journalism, five years in software development, and four additional years of casual jobs in programming.
