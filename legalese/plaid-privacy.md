## Who this covers

**Carter Pape's Plaid-API tooling** is a family of personal, non-commercial utilities operated by Carter Pape. They use [Plaid](https://plaid.com) to retrieve financial data for accounts that the operator and household members explicitly connect. They are **not** offered to the public; the only data subjects are the operator and consenting household members. This policy applies to all of them.

## What data is collected 📋

- **From Plaid:** financial-account data the user connects via Plaid Link — account and routing numbers, balances, transactions, account-holder identity, and related metadata.
- **Credentials:** Plaid client ID/secret and Plaid access tokens (these authorize data retrieval; they are operator secrets, not collected from users).
- We do **not** collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data, and we do not use third-party trackers.

## How data is used 🎯

Solely to generate the operator's own personal-finance outputs (e.g. the monthly Venmo-card summary, HSA-reimbursement tracking, monthly analytics). Data is **never** sold, rented, or shared for advertising, and is not disclosed to any third party except Plaid (as the data source) and the infrastructure the operator uses to run the tool.

## How data is stored & protected 🔒

- Secrets are held in an OS keychain or secrets manager, never in source control.
- Access requires MFA-protected accounts under the operator's Access Control Policy.
- Retained and deleted per the operator's Data Retention & Deletion Policy.

(The operator maintains the Access Control Policy and a Data Retention & Deletion Policy. These are not published, but data subjects may request a copy at the contact email below.)

## Your rights 🪪

Because data subjects are the operator and household members, any subject may, at any time, request that their connected accounts be disconnected and their data deleted. Disconnection is performed in [Plaid Portal](https://my.plaid.com) and local deletion per the retention policy. Plaid's own handling of consumer data is described at <https://plaid.com/legal/>.

## Contact

Questions or deletion requests: **<plaid.com@carterpape.com>**.
